We bring passion.
Our growing number of LEED APs understands the LEED certification process and partners with clients to realize an institution’s sustainable visions.

We are meeting the Challenge.
The 2030 Challenge, that is. Architecture 2030 established the 2030 Challenge “to achieve a dramatic reduction in the global-warming-causing greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions of the Building Sector by changing the way buildings and developments are planned, designed and constructed.” Organizations that accept the challenge, as SHW Group has done, agree to take major steps to reduce GHG.
http://www.architecture2030.org

We earned the Energy Star.
You have seen the Energy Star logo on home appliances, but did you know that schools can earn the Energy Star? In fact, the EPA just accepted the Gloria Marshall Elementary School in Spring, Texas, for the Energy Star Challenge, and the project will be displayed at the upcoming AIA National Convention.
http://www.energystar.gov
